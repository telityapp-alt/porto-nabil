Content Playground Strategy
Playground untuk HR di Asia — bukan cuma news, tapi resource hub lengkap
Core Insight
HR Portal Problem
HR teams di Asia struggle dengan info scattered across platform berbeda. Labor law ada di government sites yang susah navigate, templates di marketplace yang generic, news di media umum yang tidak contextualized untuk region. Tidak ada single source of truth untuk HR intelligence di Asia.
Traditional Pain Points
  • Google 6 government sites buat labor law per country
  • Pay $50-200 untuk HR templates yang generic (tidak Asia-specific)
  • Subscribe ke 5+ newsletters untuk regional HR news
  • Hire consultant untuk context-sensitive compliance questions
Solution
HR Asia Playground
Kami build content playground yang jadi one-stop hub untuk everything HR across Asia. Regulation vault, ready-to-use templates, weekly news dengan practitioner context, honest tool reviews, dan AI assistant yang answer dalam seconds. Free, comprehensive, region-focused.
Four Content Pillars
  • Regulation Vault: Labor law references untuk 6 countries (Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, India, Japan) dengan practical context
  • Resource Library: 18 ready-to-use templates (hiring, onboarding, performance, compliance, offboarding) written untuk solo HR teams
  • Weekly Newsletter: Asia HR Pulse — news briefs, regulation updates, curated resources. One email, high signal-to-noise
  • Free Tools: 4 live tools (Job Description Grader, HR Maturity Score, Salary Competitiveness Check, Red Flag Detector) yang deliver value dalam 30-120 seconds
Content Architecture
Multi-Layer Value Stack
Content didesain dengan layered depth: surface-level tools untuk quick wins (graders, checkers), mid-level resources untuk practical use (templates, news), dan deep-level references untuk complex decisions (regulation vault, case studies). User bisa engage di level mana pun sesuai need mereka saat itu.
Layer 1: Quick Wins (30s - 2min)
  • Job Description Grader — instant feedback on posting quality
  • Salary Competitiveness Check — benchmark offer vs market
  • Red Flag Detector — toxic culture signals di job posts
  • HR Maturity Score — assess current function capability
Layer 2: Practical Resources (5-15min)
  • Ready-to-use templates (offer letter, performance review forms, onboarding checklists)
  • Weekly news briefs — what changed and what it means untuk practice
  • Tool reviews — honest takes on HRIS, ATS, payroll platforms (no sponsors)
  • Founder × HR articles — pre-first-hire guidance untuk startup founders
Layer 3: Deep References (30min+)
  • Regulation Vault — country-by-country labor law dengan practical context
  • Talent case studies — what companies got right and wrong about people
  • HR Certifications Library — credentials that move careers
  • Community — find your people, regional peer discussions
Content Frequency
Weekly Cadence Strategy
Kami publish weekly via newsletter untuk maintain top-of-mind presence tanpa overwhelm inbox. Every Monday: 3 news items, 1 resource, 1 regulation update. Signal-to-noise ratio tinggi — no filler, no sponsored content, no affiliate links. Pure value.
Newsletter Structure (Asia HR Pulse)
  • Section 1: This Week Across Asia — 3 news briefs dengan context dan implication untuk HR practice
  • Section 2: Resource Spotlight — 1 template atau tool highlight dengan use case example
  • Section 3: Regulation Update — 1 law change atau compliance reminder untuk specific country
  • CTA: Soft pitch ke Aire (e.g., "When you're ready to automate assessments, Aire runs this workflow for you")
Playground Psychology
Kami sebut ini "playground" bukan cuma branding — ini strategic positioning. Playground implies exploration, discovery, no pressure. User datang untuk one specific need (salary check, template download), lalu explore content lain organically. Low-friction, high-value browsing experience. Tidak seperti gated content atau trial-expired platforms.
