Multi-Country Coverage Strategy
6 countries, 6 legal systems, 1 unified platform
Challenge
Asia HR Fragmentation Problem
Asia bukan monolith. Every country punya labor law berbeda, compliance requirements berbeda, cultural context berbeda. HR professional yang manage teams across region harus juggle 6+ government websites, translate legal text, dan understand local nuance. No existing platform consolidate this intelligence.
Region-Specific Pain Points
  • Indonesia: Omnibus Law complexity, severance calculation rules, BPJS compliance
  • Singapore: Employment Act tiers, foreign worker levies, CPF contribution rates
  • Malaysia: EPF/SOCSO requirements, retrenchment benefit calculation, Employment Act 1955
  • Philippines: 13th month pay, DOLE compliance, security of tenure regulations
  • India: PF/ESI thresholds, state-level labor laws (29 states), gratuity rules
  • Japan: Labor Standards Act, shakai hoken (social insurance), annual leave carryover
Solution
Unified Regulation Vault
Kami build Regulation Vault yang consolidate labor law untuk 6 countries dalam single interface. Bukan cuma cite law text — kami provide practical context, calculation examples, compliance checklists, dan recent updates. Content structure consistent across countries untuk easy navigation.
Per-Country Content Structure
  • Overview: Key employment law snapshot (working hours, leave entitlements, termination rules)
  • Statutory Benefits: Mandatory contributions (pension, health, unemployment insurance) dengan calculation examples
  • Hiring & Termination: Employment contract requirements, probation periods, severance calculation
  • Leave Policies: Annual leave, sick leave, maternity/paternity, public holidays
  • Recent Updates: Law changes dalam last 12 months dengan effective dates
  • Compliance Checklist: Actionable checklist untuk HR audit

Country-by-Country Breakdown

Kami go deep: cite specific law sections, provide calculation worksheets, link to official government portals, dan explain practical implication dengan real examples. Content written untuk solo HR atau founders yang tidak punya dedicated legal team.

Content Localization
Language & Context Strategy
Platform interface dalam English untuk cross-country accessibility, tapi content include local language terms (with English translation) where relevant. Example: "shakai hoken (social insurance)" untuk Japan, "BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (employment social security)" untuk Indonesia. Membantu user yang collaborate dengan local teams atau government agencies.
Practical Context Layer
  • Calculation examples: "For a Rp 10,000,000 monthly salary in Indonesia, severance after 3 years = ..."
  • Compliance timelines: "EPF contributions in Malaysia due by 15th of following month"
  • Penalty warnings: "Late CPF payment in Singapore: 17% per annum interest + possible prosecution"
  • Common mistakes: "Many companies misclassify Part IV vs non-Part IV employees in Singapore"
Update Cadence
Keeping Content Current
Labor law berubah frequently — new minimum wage, contribution rate adjustments, law amendments. Kami monitor official government portals dan legal updates untuk 6 countries. Changes flagged di newsletter (Asia HR Pulse) dengan effective date dan action required. Regulation Vault content updated within 7 days of official announcement.
Update Sources
  • Indonesia: Kemnaker (Ministry of Manpower) announcements, BPJS circulars
  • Singapore: MOM (Ministry of Manpower) press releases, IRAS updates
  • Malaysia: KWSP (EPF), PERKESO (SOCSO) notices, Labour Department
  • Philippines: DOLE Department Orders, SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-IBIG advisories
  • India: EPFO notifications, central & state labor department updates
  • Japan: MHLW (Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare) publications
Expansion Roadmap
Future Country Coverage
Current focus: solidify 6-country foundation dengan comprehensive content depth. Next expansion targets (based on user demand): Thailand, Vietnam, Australia. Prioritization criteria: (1) Aire customer concentration, (2) English proficiency (platform language), (3) digital HR maturity, (4) regulatory complexity (high complexity = high value content opportunity).
Why Multi-Country Matters
Regional companies (e.g., Singaporean startup expanding to Indonesia & Philippines, atau Japanese company dengan APAC operations) need HR intelligence across borders. Single-country HR platforms force them ke juggle multiple subscriptions. HR Asia positioning: the only platform yang provide unified, practitioner-focused labor law intelligence across Asia's major markets. Content breadth = competitive moat.
