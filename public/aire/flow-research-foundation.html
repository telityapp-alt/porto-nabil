85 Tahun I-O Psychology Research: What Actually Predicts Job Performance
r = .42
Structured Interviews
Sackett et al. (2022) · Meta-analysis of 100+ years employment testing
Standardized questions dengan scoring rubric yang sama untuk semua candidates. Highest validity untuk predicting job performance dibanding unstructured interviews (r = .20).
Implementasi di Aire
AI-powered conversational interviews dengan pre-defined scoring dimensions. Setiap candidate ditanya same core questions, dengan adaptive follow-ups based on responses. BARS (Behaviorally Anchored Rating Scales) rubrics untuk consistency.
r = .33
Work Sample Tests
Roth et al. (2005) · Validity and fairness of work sample assessments
Candidates solve actual job-related problems. System design untuk engineers, case studies untuk PMs, data analysis untuk Data Scientists. Highest face validity dan lowest adverse impact.
Implementasi di Aire
Assessment templates built around real work scenarios, bukan abstract puzzles. AI scores nuanced responses (system design tradeoffs, case study reasoning) dengan rubrics grounded in role competencies, bukan keyword matching.
r = .34
Situational Judgment Tests
Christian et al. (2010) · SJT validity meta-analysis
Present hypothetical work situations dan minta candidate pilih best action. Measure judgment, problem-solving, dan interpersonal skills dalam context yang realistic.
Implementasi di Aire
SJT blocks untuk leadership roles (Engineering Manager, PM) dengan scenario derived dari Amazon LPs dan Google's Project Oxygen research. AI scores considering multiple valid approaches, bukan single correct answer.
r = .31
General Cognitive Ability
Schmidt & Hunter (1998) · The validity and utility of selection methods
Logical reasoning, problem-solving, learning ability. Most generalizable predictor across jobs. Combined dengan work samples untuk highest composite validity.
Implementasi di Aire
Cognitive reasoning blocks embedded dalam role templates (Data Scientist, Analyst). Bukan IQ tests, tapi job-relevant problem solving: pattern recognition, numerical reasoning, logical inference.
Kenapa Research-Backed Templates Matter
Defensible Hiring Decisions
Waktu leadership tanya "Why candidate A over B?", lu bisa point ke specific competency scores grounded in published research, bukan gut feeling atau vague 'cultural fit'.
Legal Compliance
Structured assessments dengan job-relevant content dan consistent scoring rubrics lebih defensible secara legal dibanding unstructured interviews prone ke bias.
Lower Adverse Impact
Work samples dan cognitive tests show lower adverse impact across demographics dibanding personality tests atau unstructured interviews (Roth et al., 2005).
Higher Candidate Experience
Candidates prefer assessments yang job-relevant ("This actually tests what I'd do") vs abstract puzzles atau personality questions ("What does this have to do dengan the role?").
Composite Validity Boost
Combining multiple methods (structured interview + work sample + cognitive test) increases overall validity. Schmidt & Hunter: composite validity bisa reach .60+ (vs .20-.40 untuk single method).
