Kenapa Freemium Work untuk Assessment Platform
Zero Friction Acquisition
No credit card, no trial expiry = zero friction signup. User experience full value before seeing price. Traditional SaaS lock behind 14-day trial — user stress tentang evaluation timeline. Free forever = no pressure, explore at your own pace.
Natural Upgrade Path
User mulai dengan free tier, build assessment, send ke candidates. Waktu mereka butuh deeper signal ("Is candidate A really better than B?"), Aire offer AI deep-dive. Upgrade terjadi at decision point, bukan arbitrary trial end.
Pay-Per-Use Alignment
Hiring is spiky. Company hire 5 people bulan ini, 0 people next 3 months. Annual subscription ($1,700/year) = paying untuk months tanpa hiring. Pay-per-use ($8/assessment) = pay only when actually hiring. Perfect alignment dengan customer need.
Viral Growth via Free Tier
Free tier dengan no limits = user bisa refer peers tanpa guilt. "Try Aire, it's actually free" vs "Try this tool (tapi lu cuma punya 14 days)". Word-of-mouth acquisition way easier dengan truly free product.
Competitive Moat
Competitors (TestGorilla, HackerRank) locked into annual subscription model karena infrastructure cost tinggi. Aire bisa offer free forever karena serverless edge architecture = near-zero marginal cost. Sustainable competitive advantage.
