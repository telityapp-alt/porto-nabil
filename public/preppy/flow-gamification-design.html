Gamification Design System
6 Psychology Principles yang Drive Engagement & Retention
Loss Aversion & Streaks
Kahneman & Tversky
User lebih takut losing streak mereka daripada value dari gaining XP. Daily engagement jadi habitual untuk avoid pain dari breaking progress.
Implementasi
  • Visual streak counter di home screen
  • Push notification jam 8 malam: "Jangan break 12-day streak lo!"
  • Streak freeze power-up (1 miss allowed per minggu)
  • Social leaderboard show streak leaders
Variable Rewards
B.F. Skinner
Unpredictable reward timing trigger dopamine release. User stay engaged hoping untuk bonus berikutnya, even when none is guaranteed.
Implementasi
  • Random XP multipliers (1.5x–3x) setelah questions
  • Surprise achievement unlocks mid-session
  • Mystery reward chests (10% spawn rate)
  • Daily spin wheel dengan varying prizes
Social Proof & Competition
Cialdini's Influence
Liat progress peers normalize struggle dan validate effort. Competition drive engagement through FOMO dan status pursuit.
Implementasi
  • Weekly leaderboard (top 10 visible)
  • Counter "127 users completed this today"
  • Friend progress feed di home screen
  • Public achievement badges on profile
Immediate Feedback Loop
Csikszentmihalyi's Flow
Instant validation keep user di flow state. Waiting >2 detik untuk correctness feedback break immersion dan reduce completion rates.
Implementasi
  • Answer validation dalam <200ms
  • Animated checkmark/X on submit
  • XP counter increments immediately
  • Micro-celebrations setelah correct answers
Progressive Mastery
Vygotsky's ZPD
Adaptive difficulty keep user di Zone of Proximal Development — challenging tapi achievable. Too hard = anxiety, too easy = boredom.
Implementasi
  • Difficulty adjust based on last 10 answers
  • Unlock harder content setelah 80% accuracy
  • Spaced repetition untuk wrong answers
  • Visual skill tree shows progression path
Endowed Progress Effect
Nunes & Drèze (2006)
Pre-seeded progress bikin user feel invested sebelum mereka beneran mulai. "Already 20% done!" increase completion rate 25%.
Implementasi
  • First lesson starts at 10% progress
  • Achievement badges partially filled ("3/5 complete")
  • Onboarding quiz gives starter XP
  • Level 1 butuh cuma 50 XP (easy first win)
Technical Implementation Details
Streak System
Core Loop
UTC-based streak tracking dengan streak-freeze mechanic dan push notification triggers.
Storage: lastActiveDate, currentStreak, longestStreak, freezeCount
Logic: Reset at UTC midnight kalau gap >24h (unless freeze active)
Notification: Daily jam 8 malam local time kalau no activity yet
XP & Rewards Engine
Gamification
Variable reward system dengan weighted randomness dan multiplier stacking.
Base XP: 10 per correct answer, 0 untuk incorrect
Multiplier: 15% chance 1.5x, 8% chance 2x, 2% chance 3x
Bonus: Perfect streak (+50 XP), speed bonus (<5s = +20 XP)
Adaptive Difficulty
AI Engine
Real-time difficulty adjustment based on rolling accuracy window.
Window: Last 10 answers (sliding average)
Thresholds: <60% = easier, 60-80% = same, >80% = harder
Adjustment: Question difficulty ±1 level (5 levels total)
Social Features
Engagement
Weekly leaderboard dengan privacy controls dan anti-cheat validation.
Scope: Resets every Monday UTC 00:00
Ranking: Total XP earned minggu ini (not cumulative)
Privacy: Users bisa opt out (hidden from public board)
Achievement System
Progression
Tiered badge system dengan rarity levels dan progress tracking.
Tiers: Bronze (common), Silver (rare), Gold (epic)
Categories: Streaks, XP milestones, perfect scores, speed
Display: Progress bars ("850/1000 XP to Gold Scholar")
Notification Strategy
Retention
Context-aware push notifications dengan frequency caps dan A/B tested copy.
Triggers: Streak risk (8PM), achievement unlock, leaderboard rank
Rate Limit: Max 1 notification per 12 jam (avoid spam)
Copy A/B: Loss-framed ("Jangan lose") vs gain-framed ("Earn bonus")
